In May 2026, I was invited to present at the International Conference on the Prevention of Musculoskeletal Disorders, entitled Sustainable Musculoskeletal Disorder Prevention: New Challenges for Research and Practice.

My presentation was part of the symposium Understanding the Long-Term Consequences of Exoskeletons in MSD Prevention: A Cross-Disciplinary Perspective. It was entitled Persistent Effects of Upper-Limb Exoskeletons on Inter-Joint Coordination. It was really interesting to exchange with researchers and industrials with different and complementary perspecitves.
