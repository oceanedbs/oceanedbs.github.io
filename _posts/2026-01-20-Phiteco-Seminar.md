In January 2026, I was invited to present the documentary *Smart Arm: A Human and Technological Adventure* at the [PHITECO seminar](https://sites.google.com/site/mineurphiteco/s%C3%A9minaires-et-ateliers/phiteco-2026-techniques-handicaps-concevoir-laccessibilit%C3%A9?authuser=0), whose annual theme was “Technology and Disability: Designing for Accessibility.”

On this occasion, a screening of the documentary was organized, followed by a discussion with the students and researchers in attendance. It was a valuable opportunity to reflect on the role of assistive technologies: what they should aim to do, what they should avoid, and how they can be designed with and for the people they are intended to support.
